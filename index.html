INCREMENTO DE LOS INGRESOS


PUBLICIDAD DIRIGIDA

Is used across many industries to provide personalized offers to consumers, based on their sociodemographic characteristics, shopping behavior and other profile elements. It can be done online, but also through coupons, billing statements or any other means of communication (diagnostic analytics, prescriptive analytics).

BUSQUEDAS POR SIMILITUD

Helps when people search for jobs, hotels, secondhand cars, houses and other goods with many characteristics. Fuzzy matching pairs results that are almost fitting, like a dark blue car instead of a black one, or a hotel on a different Greek island from the one requested (prescriptive analytics).

RECOMENDACIONES PERSONALIZADA

Are the mostly widely adopted big data use in the retail sector. Based on what other customers have bought, a customer could also be interested in another product. This is also called "best next offer" recommendation. Recommendations can benefit from a much broader context, not only checking which combinations are most likely, but also, based on a very fine-grained "graph analysis," identifying a closely related peer consumer group. It can also work with people, such as when LinkedIn recommends other people to connect to. It can additionally be used with locations, based on common or complementary characteristics (descriptive analytics, prescriptive analytics).

EXPERIENCIAS LÚDICAS (GAMIFICATION)

Is a new concept, based on smartphone location data (or data from any other location-aware device). Visitors to a mall can be tempted to buy more and stay longer by tracking their movements through a mall and sending them special offers — for instance after checking in at more than 10 stores (descriptive, predictive analytics, prescriptive analytics).

MANEJO ACTIVO DE LEALTAD

Benefits from big data by extending channel reach from point of sale, Web and call center to include mobile and social capabilities. Rewards may be accrued by more than purchases; people may also earn them for being good product or brand ambassadors. Rewards may also come from more than personal contributions — social relationships may be included (descriptive analytics).

EXPERIENCIA DEL CLIENTE


EXPERIENCIAS CONSISTENTES

Is needed when customer interactions take place within both corporate systems and external systems like social media. By matching the attributes of people, they can be linked to internal customer data (predictive analytics).

ANÁLISIS DE SENTIMIENTO

Involves the tracking and interpretation of public discussions, often on social media such as Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn, to see how the general public feels about an organization (its brand, products and services) or broader themes that affect corporate performance (such as politics, large-scale events and overall social sentiment) (diagnostic analytics, descriptive analytics).

SERVICIO AL CLIENTE 360º

Has been a vision in the banking sector for a long time, taking all relevant customer indicators into account, for assessing risk or offering better service. Big data helps by enabling analysis of unstructured public data (such as social media profiles or, in a business-to-business context, analyzing news about a certain organization) and analysis of relationships between customers. Interestingly, this harks back to a traditional community approach to banking, whereby, for example, the farmer’s son would get a loan based on the credibility of his father (descriptive analytics).

SERVICIOS DE VALOR AGREGADO BASADOS EN INFORMACIÓN

Which, for example, monitor payment transactions trigger alerts relating to the availability of funds for corporate customers based on individual customers' parameters; alternatively, they provide point-of-sale information based on card activity to enable merchants to make personalized offers (prescriptive analytics).

MANTENIMIENTO PROACTIVO

Is relevant not only for manufacturing equipment but also for the products manufactured. If, through the Internet of Things, consumers of professional technology are connected, they can be informed of upcoming maintenance or expected malfunction (predictive analytics, prescriptive analytics).

PROTECCIÓN DEL INGRESO


MANTENIMIENTO PREDICTIVO DE ACTIVOS

Is the next step after preventive maintenance. Asset-intensive industries, such as manufacturing, utilities and telecommunications, benefit from outfitting their equipment with sensors that track sounds, vibrations, movements and other physical signs of upcoming malfunction or failure (predictive analytics).

OPTIMIZACIÓN DE OPERACIONES EN TIEMPO REAL

Benefits from complex event processing, combined with prescriptive analytics to do "decision management" (prescriptive analytics).

MONITOREO DEL FLUJO DE PIEZAS EN TIEMPO REAL

Is the next step after just-in-time supply chain optimization, made possible by outfitting all parts with sensors and tracking these in real time (prescriptive).

PLANEACION DE LA CONFIGURACION DE PRODUCTO

Helps by offering fast delivery times for the manufacture of millions of different product configurations. Through advanced pattern analysis, the most popular configurations can be predicted (predictive analytics, prescriptive analytics).

MODELADO DINAMICO DE REDES DE SUMINISTRO

Manufacturers are reassessing, revaluating and reimagining their product supply networks. This is being driven by new customers and new markets, but also by the use of outsourced partners (copackers, contract manufacturers, and 3PL and 4PL partners and so on). Being able to model the product supply network based on multiple variables — location, costs (fixed, variable), demand forecast accuracy, raw-material input costs, logistics costs, cost per product, risk and so on — helps determine how the supply chain for a market should be designed, and what kinds of production facility or distribution model should be employed (prescriptive analytics).

EFICIENCIA OPERACIONAL


MONITOREO PERMANENTE DE UTILIZACIÓN

Is another function of fitting products with sensors. Already, some sinks can indicate water usage. More comprehensively, the "connected car" monitors driving behavior, including driver fatigue, acts as a location-based theft alarm, notifies emergency services in the event of accident, and so forth (predictive analytics).

MEDIDORES INTELIGENTES

Communicate the consumption of gas, water and light (for example) electronically to utility companies. This information can be used for analytics — for example, by educating the general public about how to consume as little water or electricity as possible, or to identify and communicate irregular activity (business intelligence, prescriptive analytics).

SIGUIENTE MEJOR ACCION

May analyze various types of data to improve the relevance of offers, such as a customer's current activity (such as clicks through a website), current product holdings, responses to past offers, creditworthiness, personal information, personal preferences and more general information, such as how similar customers have responded to offers. A focus on gaining more "wallet share" is a top priority for banks. (prescriptive analytics).

NUEVAS LINEAS DE NEGOCIO


DETECCIÓN DE FRAUDES

Is a recurring theme across all industries. Fraud often occurs in patterns, consisting of many small transactions each just below a certain threshold, or the use of certain language in text, and it can even be detected by voice analytics. Because fraud is relatively rare, and fraud patterns are always changing, big data and anomaly detection can help improve fraud rates and reduce false positives (descriptive analytics, predictive analytics).

AUDITORIAS DE CUMPLIMIENTO

Can benefit from automated discovery techniques and content analysis (descriptive analytics).

ANALISIS PREDICTIVO DE LA CALIDAD DEL PRODUCTO

Uses big data analytics to determine the composition of products and predict product quality. Wine-tasting scores, for instance, can be predicted using chemical analysis (predictive analytics).

MONITOREO DEL PERSONAL

Through sensors can be crucial in hazardous environments, including air quality, employee productivity, anomalous behavior and so forth (descriptive analytics).
